About SEACET

S.E.A College of Engineering and Technology, established under the prestigious SEA Group of Institutions, is located in Bangalore, Karnataka. Affiliated to VTU Belagavi and accredited with NAAC B++ grade, SEACET offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Technology, and Management.

The college is approved by AICTE and committed to imparting quality professional education incorporating values and ethics through research, innovation, and academic excellence.

🎯 Our Vision

To achieve academic excellence through OBE, provide conducive environment for holistic development, imbibe research & innovation eco-system, and bridge academia-industry gap.
