💡 Interview Tips

📝 Resume Tips
Keep your resume to one page. Highlight projects, internships, and skills relevant to the role. Use action verbs and quantify achievements. Include your CGPA, technical skills, and links to GitHub/LinkedIn. Proofread carefully for grammar and formatting.
🧮 Aptitude Preparation
Practice quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal ability daily. Use platforms like IndiaBix, PrepInsta, and GeeksforGeeks. Focus on speed and accuracy. Most IT companies have aptitude as the first round - clearing this is crucial.
💻 Technical Round
Master Data Structures & Algorithms — arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, dynamic programming. Practice on LeetCode (aim for 200+ problems). Understand OOP concepts, DBMS, OS, and CN fundamentals. Be ready to code on a whiteboard or shared editor.
🤝 HR Round
Prepare your introduction, strengths/weaknesses, and "Why this company?" Research the company culture and recent news. Be honest, confident, and show enthusiasm. Prepare examples using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
👥 Group Discussion
Stay updated on current affairs, technology trends, and social issues. Speak confidently but don't dominate. Listen to others and build on their points. Make eye contact, use structured arguments, and conclude effectively.
🏆 Coding Interview
Think aloud during the interview. Clarify the problem before coding. Start with brute force, then optimize. Discuss time and space complexity. Test your code with edge cases. Practice mock interviews on Pramp or InterviewBit.
